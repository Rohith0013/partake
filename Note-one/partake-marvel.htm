Marvel Crunchy Super Hero Sprinkle Mini Cookie Snack Packs

When hunger strikes, our mini cookie snack packs, in collaboration with Marvel, will save the day. With just the right amount of vanilla and a super satisfying crunch, our Crunchy Super Hero Sprinkle Mini Cookie Snack Packs are filled with red and blue sprinkles. Vegan, gluten-free, and non-GMO, these anytime snacks are free of the top 9 allergens and full of cheer—safe for school and ideal for sharing. Each box contains ten (10) individually-wrapped snack packs.
